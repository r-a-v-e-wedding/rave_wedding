FAQ

Can anyone come to crash? Can I bring other people ?

Anyone coming to crash should be cleared by a wedding guest who sends you this link / invited you to crash in the first place. If you'd like to bring someone else who that wedding guest does not know, make sure that it's been OK'd by the guest who invited you, and make sure that they fill out the RSVP form found on this page.


When can I arrive? When should I leave ?

Anytime after 10pm on Saturday September 7th. Please aim to depart before sunset on Sunday.


Does it cost anything ?

No, but we will be accepting donations at the door to help us cover costs as this is a high production event. You are also strongly encouraged to bring something to share with all of the other wedding ravers in the spirit of community, love, mirth, etc.

COME TO CONTRIBUTE NOT TO CONSUME

We ask that all who are coming up to crash recognize that this is not an open festival for you to come and get sloshed at, but a fun, special after party for a celebration of love.

Please come with the intention to contribute a certain amount of energy to helping make the event more special. This could be done in a variety of ways:

  • Helping with tear down on Sunday
  • Bringing something with the intention of sharing it with those around you! Edible? Experiential? Visual? Pedagogical? (Not allowed: Microbial)
  • Donating upon entrance to help cover costs of the wedding
