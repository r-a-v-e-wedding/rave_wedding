LODGING

Camp Wingate can accommodate up to 400+ campers! We will be about half that, so there is plenty of space to sleep comfortably on-site the entire weekend.

Cabins :

  • The camp has about 20 cabins that can sleep 10-20 people in bunks.
  • All cabins have fully functional bathrooms and sinks.
  • Some cabins have showers, and some have a shower house next door.
  • Some cabins will be closer to the festivities, while others are further.
  • Most bunk beds can be pushed together to create double-size beds.

Reserving :

  • After RSVPs close, we will assign groups of cabins based on family/friend groups (and other details you provide in the RSVP).
  • From there, you will be free to change your cabin assignment at your discretion.

Other accommodations :

  • There is limited space for tents if you would prefer to stay this way. If so, please indicate your desire to tent in the RSVP.
  • If you prefer not to stay on the camp: Cottages, BnBs, Hotels, Motels, can be found in the neighbouring town of Saint-Adolphe-d‚ÄôHoward. If you wish to do so, we encourage you to plan ahead.
